Green electricity deduction when installing solar cells

From 1 January 2021, you can deduct 15% of the cost of installing solar cells. The new tax reduction for green technology applies to both materials and installation. The contribution is made directly on the final invoice. Byhmgard applies for payment of the tax reduction from the Swedish Tax Agency when the work is completed and paid for.


It is important that you as a customer understand the rules regarding tax reduction for green technology works. The tax reduction covers some, but not all costs. Contact Byhmgard and we can clearly explain and answer all your questions.


Installation of green technology can give a maximum tax reduction of SEK 50,000 per person per year. You need to have paid enough tax during the year to be able to use the full amount. Tax reduction for Green Technology or "Green Deduction" is independent of other root and box deductions. This means that you can be granted a green tax reduction as well as root and root.


We are happy to answer questions. Please read more on the Swedish Tax Agency's website about how tax reduction for green technology works.
