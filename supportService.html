FAQ Battery storage and support services

Generally

What is a battery warehouse?

A battery bank is one or more batteries connected to an inverter. The latter converts the direct current into alternating current which can be used for various purposes. A small battery storage can consist of single batteries that, according to the manufacturer, are stacked on top of each other. A medium-sized battery warehouse means a cabinet that contains inverters and battery warehouses. A large battery warehouse can either be a container solution, or be many cabinets with batteries and inverters inside. For large battery stocks, we usually offer the necessary distribution center and in some cases grid station, where applicable.

What can a battery storage be used for?

A battery storage can, depending on the make, be used for several different purposes: i Emergency power / backup power, i.e. offer power in the event of a power outage for a limited time ii Island operation where power outages occur for a longer time, or where the battery storage is to be used as off-grid iii Support Servicetogether with Svenska Kraftnät, where the battery "helps" the Swedish electricity grid with imbalances iv So-called "Peak Shaving", which means that power peaks are cut down in order to lower the need for the main fuse level, or to avoid the main fuse tripping at a certain level v Electricity price arbitrage, where the battery storage is charged with cheap electricity during the cheap hours of the day, and then sold during the expensive hours of the day vi Solar balancing, where electricity from the surplus of solar cell production is saved into the battery storage and then used during the evening

Can a battery store be used simultaneously for all areas of use?

No, you can e.g. not perform electricity price arbitrage or support services against Svenska Kraftnät in the event of a power outage. Our equipment requires an internet connection and the inverters in some cases require AC power from the mains (e.g. power supply from the house's incoming electricity). When the support service for Svenska Kraftnät is activated, the battery storage cannot perform other tasks at the same time, but it is possible to schedule tasks: i Power peak creation / Peak Shaving between 07:00-18:00 ii support service between 18:00-07:00

What fuses are available for villas and what size battery can be used?

Ett batterilager för en villa eller mindre bostadsrätt behöver dimensioneras efter den huvudsäkring som fastigheten har. Huvudsäkringens storlek finns angiven på de fakturor som skickas ut av elnätägare respektive elbolag. Lilla villalagret på 10kW passar en villa som har 16A i huvudsäkring. Proffslagret passar den som redan har eller kan säkra upp till 63A. Kontakta Byhmgard om du behöver hjälp med dimensionering. Självklart kan man ha högre säkringar än 63A och de kallas då för effektabonnemang. Kontakta din elnätägare så kan de berätta mer. Nedan listas de säkringsnivåer och säkringsfärger som är vanliga i villor: Grå - 16A - 11,40 kW Blå - 20A - 13,80 kW Gul - 25A - 17,25 kW Svart - 35A - 24,15kW Vit - 50A - 34,50kW Koppar - 63A - 43,47kW
